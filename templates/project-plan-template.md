## 1. Chunking Assessment (MANDATORY)

Before planning, assess complexity:

```bash
./scripts/chunk-analyzer.sh "<brief-task-description>"
```

| Complexity | Score | Action |
|------------|-------|--------|
| Simple | 1-3 | Direct execution |
| Moderate | 4-6 | Split into 2-3 subtasks |
| Complex | 7-10 | Use chunk-orchestrator.sh |
